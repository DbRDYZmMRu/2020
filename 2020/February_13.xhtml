Entries:

         A sterile culture lacking novelty at its base is a misconception of acute discerning of the true needs of being. The inability of a people to understand the ever encompass of spatial reality morphing, is the lack of good archetypal characters with viable roles that fulfil the sincere outcry for existential fulfilment. The pulling away from stories that inhibit the subjugation of natural phenomenon at its base level is to fulfil the urging need for acceptance in a world that constantly divides and labels.

I’ll be reading Moonwalking with Einstein, a stray from proposed intentions, a foray to much needed mental juice for this life-long inquisition.
