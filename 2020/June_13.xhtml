Devotional: LIFE IS A BATTLEFIELD

Poem of the Day: The Pleasures of Friendship by Stevie Smith

Collected Poems of Maya Angelou:

Forgive

Entries:

         I stopped journaling, I really don’t know why, I guess I suck at it, maybe not, I feel washed out, like I’m less of my authentic self. I guess the least I’ve felt this way is when writing; my songs. Maybe I can experience this also when journaling, I’ll be taking out time for this.

         God is a construct.

         Thinking back to my days in the hostel, the day Papa remarked my being as useless. Well, now am a burgeoning scientist, and I’m proud to say, I stand for something of pragmatic usefulness.

         She wields a trident, I swoop my scythe, she is the love of my life.
