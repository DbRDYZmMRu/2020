Devotional: OPINIONS ARE LIKE . . .

Poem of the Day: Waste Land Limericks by Wendy Cope

Entries:

         Today is a day of looking in, I woke up to the delight of Dagny’s rendition of some of her favourite songs, to her loved ones and family. Sleeping to the Ocean Eyes album by Owl City, captured the sensation of what was left of the fable dream I’d had earlier. Disconnecting from the headphones, I woke up to the dreamy song of birds in harmony with mine, the song in my head. Beautiful 21 Pilots... I’ll be leaving the jacket behind. 

         The improvison: do not narrow your cognition, avoid acts at aim of self-aberration.
