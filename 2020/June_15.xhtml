Devotional: LISTENING ACCOMPLISHES MORE THAN SPEAKING

Poem of the Day: from Prayer Before Birth by Louis MacNeice

Entries:

         What a day, listening to a soothing beat as I type this away, how the internet slows down my set-out work for the day is infuriating and insufferable, I’ll be dismissing its access today, instead sticking with my good-ol keep it in the look-up sheet.

         I learned today that the Enlightenment is part of the Renaissance era, a period in the eighteenth century, and the Renaissance started in the Middle Ages in the fourteenth century up until the seventeenth century. The Middle Ages started in the twelfth century ending in the sixteenth. 

         The greatest thing I learned the past week is that most times I cry why . . . this dirt in my eye? It’s actually been my eye lashes, all this time? Darn! Thank goodness I didn’t lose it.
