         I had a fight with Jessica yesterday, I went off saying all the things I didn’t understand, and how sometimes I really lose sense of purpose out of enthusiasm. I later sent a mail saying I’m sorry.

         I miss journaling, but the electricity has taken a dive for the sea level, I hope it its steady enough for me to continue with my work.

Caged away a bird, peeled.

It had perched on my sill.

Beak clack! Clack!

Clack! Clack!

Strange for a call

Now out park.

Tap! At the sash

Clack! At its glam

It pares. Pares it seems

Din here, din there; on seam

In beams on its shimmery plumage

I wrest him in my hands cup siege.

And now in a mirror cage

A singing page he stages

Beak clack! clack!

Clack! Clack!
