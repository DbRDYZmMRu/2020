         The important thing to note in my writing, my works is that apart from it going out to inspire, lifting the soul, it also sets out to be a reference, a resource to drawn on for future works.

         I thought to include this here, it’s a quote from yesterday’s musical introduction passage:

enthusiastically studied folk songs of all nations, because the folk song is the source of all true melodies: the wellspring at which one must repeatedly renew and refresh oneself . . . ’


         Another quote from today’s musical introduction passage:

‘I appear at times merry and in good heart, talk, too, before others quite reasonably, and it looks as if I felt, too, God knows how well within my skin. Yet the soul maintains its deathly sleep and the heart bleeds from a thousand wounds.’

 

‘Sleep! Sweet Sleep!

Although, next to death, there is nothing that so much resembles you,

on this couch I proclaim you welcome!’
